Ethereum

Cryptoart tokenized on the Ethereum blockchain.

I currently have three primary smart contracts on Ethereum built around cryptoart. This was partially for diversity reasons, but also as a result of requiring separate ERC-721 and ERC-1155 contracts, as my first contract did not support convenient multiple editions. These can be found in various market and platforms across the crypto space, including Opensea, Mintbase, Rarible, and Cargo.

The Black Fractal Collection

Contract 01: The Black Fractal Contract Address: 0x634573a883cdc8fd7791a7fec456bee0d5d87ce5 Token: BKFX Created: Mintbase.io Description: This contract is my primary cryptoart contract, and contains four subcategories; Entity, Mandala, Abstract, and Corruption. Entity Series revolves around the pareidolic psychedelic visions of beings in complex geometry. Mandala Series is based around radial geometry. Abstract is just that, a more pure and unformed abstraction of geometry. Corruption is derived from taking glorious structures, such as cathedrals and government buildings, and warping them is to hellish versions of their former selves.

Trip Store Collection

Contract 02: Trip Store Contract Address: 0x47f25D15D68260c7E572939a253C7eAdd953c9D0 Token: TRYPT Created: Rarible.com Description: This contract was built to be the main store for my social currency $TRIP. The NFTs on sale here will be conducted exclusively in $TRIP. They range from editions of 1/1 to multiples. This may change.

ネオンの伝説 Collection

Contract 03: ネオンの伝説 COLLECTION Contract Address: 0x001bA7dC2DdFA893C17b6b478c9853B83fC8594C Token: NDEN Created: Rarible.com Description: This contract was built for more a more eclectic project. The idea was to take short screen grabs from cyberpunk anime and glitch the ever loving glitch out of them. The separate contract was partially to avoid any future potential copyright issues, though as artistic interpretation, this was unlikely a realistic concern.
